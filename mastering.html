Frequently Asked Questions

What file format should I send? +

Please send your tracks as WAV files at 24-bit, 44.1kHz or higher. Make sure your mix has at least -3dB to -6dB of headroom (no clipping or limiting on the master channel). If you're unsure, feel free to reach out and I can guide you through the export process.

How long does mastering take? +

Single tracks are typically delivered within 48-72 hours. EPs take around 72 hours, and full albums usually take 5-7 days. If you need a rush delivery, contact me and I'll do my best to accommodate your timeline.

What's included in the mastering? +

My mastering process includes EQ balancing, compression, stereo enhancement, limiting for optimal loudness, and preparation for streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.). You'll receive your track in WAV and MP3 formats, mastered to industry standards.

Do you offer stem mastering? +

Yes! Stem mastering is available for an additional fee. This involves mastering grouped stems (drums, bass, synths, vocals, etc.) separately for more control over the final sound. Contact me for custom pricing on stem mastering projects.

What if I'm not happy with the results? +

I offer up to 2 revisions on single tracks and unlimited revisions on EP and Album packages. I want you to be completely satisfied with the final product. We'll work together until your track sounds exactly how you envision it.

Can you master for vinyl? +

Absolutely! Vinyl mastering requires different technical considerations than digital mastering. I can prepare your tracks specifically for vinyl cutting, ensuring they translate well to the physical format. This is included in the Album Package or available as an add-on for other packages.
